Pasta Bolognese

Description

Everyone needs a great everyday Spaghetti Bolognese recipe! The Bolognese Sauce is rich, thick and has beautiful depth of flavour. It is perfect for a quick midweek meal but even better if you can simmer it for a couple of hours! Serve with garlic bread, garden salad with Italian dressing, and tiramisu for dessert for the full experience.

Ingredients

  • Beef (or your fav vegan alternative like Impossible Ground)
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaves
  • Sugar
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Chopped nion
  • Minced garlic
  • Red wine (optional)
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Pasta of your choice (I like to use fettuchini or ziti)

Steps

  1. Saute the garlic and onion for about 3 minutes
  2. Brown the beef or vegan alternative (2-5 minutes) then add red wine (optional) and reduce
  3. Add the other ingredients (aside from the pasta), stir and simmer for 20 minutes
  4. Make your pasta to your taste while simmering
  5. Toss the pasta in the sauce for an authentic experience and thick sauce
